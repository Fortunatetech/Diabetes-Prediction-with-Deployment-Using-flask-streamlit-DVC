About This Project

Explore our Diabetes Prediction Project, designed to provide accurate predictions for diabetes risk. This tool empowers individuals to assess their diabetes risk based on their health data.

Key Features

  • Input Health Data and Get Real-time Predictions
  • Interactive Dashboard
  • Easy-to-Use Interface

How to Use

Follow these simple steps to use the Diabetes Prediction App:

  1. Navigate to the prediction page by clicking the "Prediction Page" button above.
  2. Enter your health data as requested in the form.
  3. Click the "Get Prediction" button to receive real-time diabetes risk predictions.

Info About Input Features

The features required for diabetes risk prediction are as follows:

  • Pregnancies: Number of pregnancies (integer).
  • Glucose: Fasting blood glucose level (mg/dL) (float).
  • Blood Pressure: Resting blood pressure (mm Hg) (integer).
  • Skin Thickness: Skinfold thickness (mm) (integer).
  • Insulin: Fasting serum insulin level (mu U/ml) (integer).
  • BMI (Body Mass Index): Current BMI (kg/mÂ²) (float).
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: Diabetes pedigree function score (float).
  • Age: Age in years (integer).
